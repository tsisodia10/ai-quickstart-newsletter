Lemonade Stand Assistant + Guardrails

AI that serves customers — safely, every time.

Showcased live on the Red Hat Summit 2026 stage, the Lemonade Stand Assistant demonstrates how to deploy a production-ready AI customer service chatbot with enterprise-grade safety guardrails baked in.

CORE CAPABILITIES
  • Deploys an AI-powered customer service assistant on OpenShift AI
  • Enforces family-friendly, policy-compliant interactions via guardrails
  • Shows how guardrails integrate with real LLM deployments as a first-class feature
  • Demonstrates responsible AI patterns that scale to any enterprise use case
